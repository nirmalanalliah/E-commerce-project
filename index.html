ABOUT US

Spices are dried parts of a plant, other than the leaves, used for seasoning and flavoring a recipe, but not used as the main ingredient . The green leafy parts of plants used in this way are considered herbs . Spices can be used in various ways in cooking, often alone or in conjunction with herbs . Some examples of spices include cinnamon, cardamom, allspice, and cloves . When cooking with spices, it's best to grind your own spices immediately before using them, rather than using spices that are already ground . Ground spices can last for up to three years if stored properly and whole dry spices for four years . Spices last longer when stored in a cool, dry location . India is the world's largest consumer, producer, and exporter of spices . They even created the Indian Institute of Spices Research devoted to the study of spices . I hope this information helps you!
